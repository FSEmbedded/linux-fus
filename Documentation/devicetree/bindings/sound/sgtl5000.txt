* Freescale SGTL5000 Stereo Codec

Required properties:
- compatible : "fsl,sgtl5000".

- reg : the I2C address of the device

- clocks : the clock provider of SYS_MCLK

Optional properties:
- mono2both : Instead of just playing mono sound on the left channel, copy
              it to both channels

Example:

codec: sgtl5000@0a {
	compatible = "fsl,sgtl5000";
	reg = <0x0a>;
	clocks = <&clks 150>;
	mono2both;
};
